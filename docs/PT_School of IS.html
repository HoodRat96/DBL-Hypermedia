School of Innovation Sciences

Information meetings for Bachelor and (pre-)Master students

Regularly, information meetings are organized where information is provided about matters that are important for students. For example start meetings for first year Bachelor students or (pre-)Master students, study contract information meetings, general question times, and specific information meetings about regulations and minors.

Students are automatically assigned to the information / course code of their program; they can also subscribe to such a course code in Osiris, enabling to automatically receive an e-mail when new information is provided.

The course codes are:

Course code
0I001 Information of the study program PB-phase (1st years)
0I002 Information of the study program B-phase (2nd years)
0I003 Information of the study program B-phase (3rd years)
0I004 Information of the study program pre-Master HTI
0I005 Information of the study program pre-Master IS
0I006 Information of the study program MSc HTI
0I007 Information of the study program MSc IS
