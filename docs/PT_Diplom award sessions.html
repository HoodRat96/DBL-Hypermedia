Diplom award sessions

When you have finished you study program of the first year (also called propedeuse) or the Bachelor, and you meet the requirements of exam committee. 
In Osiris you can easily subscribe yourself for the upcoming examination session. You can do this by clicking on Progress and then Qualification request. The deadline to do so is 20 days before the date of the exam. 

If a student did not meet all requirements, the student should delay his/her registration until the next exam meeting.

Finalisation First Year

The ceremony for the propedeutic certificate takes place publicly, once per year and only for those students who met the requirements for the propedeutic exam within one year.

The ceremonies for Sustainable Innovation and Psychology & Technology are usually held in the IPO-building. The ceremony is followed by a drink in the Internaat, offered by  the department of Industrial Engineering & Innovation Sciences (IE&IS).

Propedeutic ceremonies Bachelor Sustainable Innovation (BSI) and Bachelor Psychology &Technology (BPT) will be announced at 'Graduation and examination dates' of your bachelor program.

Finalisation Bachelor

The ceremony for the Bachelor certificates takes place publicly, twice per year (provided a minimum number of candidates). The ceremony usually takes place at 16.00h in the Filmzaal in the Zwarte Doos and is followed by a reception offered by the department of Industrial Engineering & Innovation Sciences (IE&IS).

Ceremonies for Bachelor certificates Technische Innovatiewetenschappen (Major Psychology & Technology and Major Sustainable Innovation) will be announced at 'Graduation and examination dates' of your bachelor program.

Finalisation Master

The diploma award sessions start at 16:00hrs in the Blue Room of the Auditorium. The diploma award session can be called off in case of less than 10 participants.

De dates of the final examinations can be found under 'Graduation and examination dates', including the corresponding diploma award sessions. The date of the final presentation of your MSc thesis in one week before the final examination date, the date of registration is one day before the final examination date.
